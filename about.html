About eTrain etc (eTraineetc, LLC)

Our company was started based on a real passion for improving safety and healthcare education. Our team has been developing creative and engaging training for over a decade. With offices in Tampa, FL, Pittsburgh, PA and Saratoga Springs, NY, eTrain etc (eTrainetc, LLC) can quickly become a strategic partner in your training initiatives.

Our success has been attributed to our experience and commitment to our partners' success. We strive to become a key resource in all your training goals. We leverage an authoring strategy to all our development initiatives that helps reduce timelines and long-term expense. Our team includes medical device training resources, subject matter experts (Healthcare professionals), 3D engineers, 3D artists, and curriculum and instructional design professionals.

Mission and Goals:

To provide innovative training solutions that focus on improving safety and quality with increased competence and confidence; mitigating risk while reducing overall cost.

We excel in providing:

  • Fast, Flexible Learning Styles (micro-learning)
  • Adaptive learning styles and assessments
  • Interactive learning with your content
  • Clear and precise learning objectives
  • Realistic and immersive environments
  • Practice opportunities for proper skill acquisition
  • Critical thinking simulations with actions and responses
  • Diagnostic measures and feedback
  • Data analytics relating to simulation experiences and records
  • Current evidence-based information
